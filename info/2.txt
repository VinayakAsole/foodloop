For a Final Year Project, don't just build a simple food-selling website. Build a complete "Community Food Sharing & Marketplace Platform" with real-time inventory, AI features, location services, and a Progressive Web App (PWA). This will look much stronger during project evaluation and viva.

Project Title

FoodLoop: AI-Powered Community Food Marketplace & Food Waste Reduction Platform

Problem Statement

Many households prepare excess food that goes unused and is wasted. At the same time, students, bachelors, workers, and low-income individuals need affordable homemade meals. There is no dedicated platform that connects these groups while tracking food availability in real time.

Objective
Reduce food waste.
Generate income for households.
Provide affordable homemade meals.
Enable real-time food inventory tracking.
Support food donation when meals remain unsold.
